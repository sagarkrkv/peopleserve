What is People Serve?

PeopleServe is a unique platform where you can find people who can perform any task for you,and where people can find out whats required in their neighborhood and perform them to earn quick bucks.

What does it cost?

For people creating ads,it costs whatever price you determine with the hiree.For people searching for tasks it costs you nothing.
PeopleServe presently doesnt charge you for its service

Why is PeopleServe so exciting?

Its a very unique concept being introduced for probably the first time in India.It provides an avenue for students,retired employees,housewives,etc i.e anyone interested in earning money in their free time.

It also provides a good oppurtunity for people who are busy and or unable to perform some task,to find people who are willing to do it for a nominal fee
